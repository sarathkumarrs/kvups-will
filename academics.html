SYSTEMATIC ACADEMICS ACTIVITIES


CLUB

ACTIVITIES

All students are members of any two clubs of their interest and a total of 17 clubs are actively functioning in the school campus. The teachers introduce the programmes of each club to the students at the initial days of the school year and the students are registered in various clubs of their choices. The teachers are convenors of various clubs and two special periods are allotted for the club activities every week. The following are the clubs that function currently: 1.GandhiDarsan 2. Vidhyarangam Kala SahityaVedi 3. Readers club 4. Health club 5. Work experience 6. Farming club 7. Environment club 8. Forestry club 9. Sports and adventures club 10. Philatelic club 11. Malayalam club 12. English club 13. Science club 14. Mathematics club 15. Social science club 16.Hindi club 17. Energy club 18. Road safety club 19. Leo club Each club is allotted a specific corner and periods.


STAR OF

THE CLASS

The school parliament led by the elected student prime minister includes all the class leaders club leaders and SRG nominated members. The parliament consists of various ministers and the prime minister and meets once a week to organize the functionings. The speaker calls for the meetings. The school parliament helps the students to get acquainted with and gather practice in the democratic principles and improve leadership skills and also to ensure active participation of students in other school activities. The videos and records of our school parliament was submitted to the department of education through the MIKAVU programme conducted by the SSA in 2009 and was instructed to be implemented in other schools under the name BALASABHA.


ANNUAL

PLANS

The annual plan which is prepared before the beginning of the academic year by the PTA and SRG helps in the systematic functioning of the school..


STUDENTS

GRADING

Students grading which is done during the initial days of the academic year helps to assess the academic standard of each student and to organize the learning programs accordingly and subsequent changes are analysed.


CONTINUOUS

EVALUATION

The continuous evaluation and unit tests after the completion of each units helps to analyse how fruitfully the student absorbs the lesson and what all skills the student should attain by taking remedial lessons etc.


STUDENTS

ENCYCLOPEDIA

Students are encouraged to record facts and bits of knowledge which they gather from both inside and outside the classrooms in a book titled ‘students encyclopedia’, which will be useful for them in the future and has received wide acceptance . A circular put forward by the DPE in 2016 has been the main impetus behind the endeavour.

ABOUT US

K V U P S is an aided school established in 1964 at Palode sub-district, Trivandrum, which faced the threat of shutting down due to the lack of students in 1980. Now this school is one of the most commonly studied public schools in Palode sub-district.
