WELCOME TO

KVUPS PANGODE

K V U P S is an aided school established in 1964 at Palode sub-district, Trivandrum, which faced the threat of shutting down due to the lack of students in 1980. Now this school is one of the most commonly studied public schools in Palode sub-district. A concise details about the school’s current activities are given below.

OUR

MISSION

Our mission is to play a major role in education sector by providing a quality education through strict discipline based on moral values to achieve this, the harmonious co-operation of the parents, teachers and students are inevitable the satisfied parents, educationaly enlightened scholars, professionaly skilled teachers and a highly principled management are the highlighted features of this institution.

OUR

VISION

Our vision is to empower students to accure, demonstrate, articulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them, as a life long learners, to participate in and contribute to the global world and practice. The core value of the school is :respect,tolerance, inclution and excellence.

OUR FACILITIES


SMART CLASSROOMS


We provide an international facility to enrich our student's skills related to technology and academy. All the teachers are trained to classes by smartclassroom and pupils are very interested to attend those classes that develop their all over perfomance.


COMPUTER LAB


Our computer lab is well furnished and equip oriented. Specially trained teachers are leading each classes.


LIBRARY


One special period in every week is allotted for library and a concerned teacher leads the students to the library. Students are given details regarding the distribution of books. The library equipped with thousands of books has granted the students lifetime membership. The open library is accessible to the public after school hours and during holidays.
